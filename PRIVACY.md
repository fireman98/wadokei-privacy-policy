# Privacy Policy for Wadokei

**Last Updated:** April 6, 2026

Wadokei is a Traditional Japanese Time (JTT) application for Wear OS, developed by Bence Bujnoczki. This app is provided as a free service and is intended for use as is.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

Wadokei does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or usage statistics. Your privacy is a priority, and the app is designed to function entirely on your Wear OS device without requiring any external accounts or internet connectivity for its core features.

## 2. Location Data

The app requires access to your device's location (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION and ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION) to calculate accurate sunrise and sunset times. These times are necessary to compute the seasonal intervals for JTT.

- On-Device Processing: All calculations are performed locally.
- Privacy: Your location coordinates are never shared with third parties, uploaded to servers, or stored anywhere other than the app's internal memory on your device.

## 3. System Permissions

- WAKE_LOCK: This permission is used to allow the JTT clockwork to update your watch face complications and UI accurately in the background. It ensures the CPU can complete time calculations even when the screen is off, preventing the clock from falling out of sync.

## 4. Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## 5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this policy periodically for any changes.
